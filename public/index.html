API Endpoints

Rales Engine is an API that exists to expose business intelligence. This allows a user to interact with various endpoints through the browser. These include endpoints for the Merchants, Customers, Invoices, Items, Invoice Items and Transactions databases related to searches by attributes, by their associations, and endpoints related to business intelligence/analysis
